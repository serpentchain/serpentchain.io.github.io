An All-Photonic Spectral-Hexadecimal Computing Architecture

Feasibility, Materials, and Design

Adam Androulidakis

serpentchain@gmail.com · 1 June 2022 · Conceptual draft

Abstract

Examines the feasibility of an all-photonic computing architecture that performs hexadecimal logic directly in the optical-wavelength domain, with no intermediary electronic components and no optical-to-electronic-to-optical conversion.
